# Chapter I - General Provisions

## Overview

Chapter I establishes the foundational framework of the AI Act, defining its purpose, scope, key definitions, and the AI Office's role in implementation.

## Articles in This Chapter

### [Article 1 - Subject Matter](article-01.md)

Defines the overall purpose and regulatory scope of the AI Act.

**Key points:**
- Harmonized rules for AI systems
- Protection of fundamental rights
- Support for innovation and internal market

**Related recitals:** (1), (2), (8)

---

### [Article 2 - Scope](article-02.md)

Clarifies who and what the regulation applies to.

**Key points:**
- Territorial scope (providers, deployers, third countries)
- Exclusions (military, defense, national security)
- Relationship with sector-specific laws

**Related recitals:** (3), (9), (10), (11)

---

### [Article 3 - Definitions](article-03.md)

Provides 60+ definitions of key terms used throughout the regulation.

**Critical definitions:**
- AI system
- Provider, deployer, distributor
- High-risk AI system
- General-purpose AI model
- Risk, harm, reasonably foreseeable misuse

**Related recitals:** (12), (13), (14)

---

### [Article 4 - AI Office](article-04.md)

Establishes the European Artificial Intelligence Office within the Commission.

**Key responsibilities:**
- Supervising general-purpose AI models
- Supporting Member States
- Monitoring AI Act implementation
- International cooperation

**Related recitals:** (118), (119)

## Key Takeaways

!!! summary "Chapter I Essentials"
    
    **For AI Providers:**
    - Understand the broad territorial reach (Article 2)
    - Familiarize yourself with key definitions (Article 3)
    - Know that the AI Office oversees GPAI models (Article 4)
    
    **For AI Deployers:**
    - Check if you fall within scope (Article 2)
    - Understand "deployer" definition (Article 3)
    - Be aware of the AI Office's supervisory role (Article 4)
    
    **For Legal Advisors:**
    - Article 1 sets the interpretive framework
    - Article 2's exclusions are narrowly construed
    - Article 3 definitions are used consistently throughout
