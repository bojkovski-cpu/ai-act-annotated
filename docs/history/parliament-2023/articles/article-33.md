Where the Commission ascertains that a notified body does not meet or no longer meets the requirements for its notification , it shall inform the notifying Member State accordingly and request it to take the necessary corrective measures, including suspension or withdrawal of the notification if necessary. Where the Member State fails to take the necessary corrective measures, the Commission may, by means of an implementing act, suspend, restrict or withdraw the designation. That implementing act shall be adopted in accordance with the examination procedure referred to in Article 74(2). Amendment 436 Proposal for a regulation
