# Article 60: .

Before placing on the market or putting into service a high-risk AI system referred to in Article 6(2) the provider or, where applicable, the authorised representative shall register that system in the EU database referred to in Article 60 , in accordance with Article 60(2); Amendment 479 Proposal for a regulation
