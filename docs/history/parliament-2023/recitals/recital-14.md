# Recital 14

‘safety component of a product or system’ means, in line with Union harmonisation law listed in Annex II, a component of a product or of a system which fulfils a safety function for that product or system , or the failure or malfunctioning of which endangers the health and safety of persons; Amendment 178 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 15
