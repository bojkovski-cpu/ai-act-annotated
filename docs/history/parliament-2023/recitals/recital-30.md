# Recital 30

‘validation data’ means data used for providing an evaluation of the trained AI system and for tuning its non-learnable parameters and its learning process, among other things, in order to prevent underfitting or overfitting; whereas the validation dataset is a separate dataset or part of the training dataset, either as a fixed or variable split; Amendment 186 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 33
