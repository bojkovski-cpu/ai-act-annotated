# Recital 39

‘publicly accessible space’ means any publicly or privately owned physical place accessible to the public, regardless of whether certain conditions for access may apply , and regardless of the potential capacity restrictions ; Amendment 196 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 41
