# Article 56: Establishment of the

European Artificial Intelligence Board 1. A ‘European Artificial Intelligence Board’ (the ‘Board’) is established. 2. The Board shall provide advice and assistance to the Commission in order to: (a) contribute to the effective cooperation of the national supervisory authorities and the Commission with regard to matters covered by this Regulation; (b) coordinate and contribute to guidance and analysis by the Commission and the national supervisory authorities and other competent authorities on emerging issues across the internal market with regard to matters covered by this Regulation; (c) assist the national supervisory authorities and the Commission in ensuring the consistent application of this Regulation.
