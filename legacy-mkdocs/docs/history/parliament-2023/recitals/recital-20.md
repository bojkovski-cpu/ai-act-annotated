# Recital 20

‘conformity assessment’ means the process of demonstrating whether the requirements set out in Title III, Chapter 2 of this Regulation relating to an AI system have been fulfilled; Amendment 181 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 22
