# Recital 24

Any processing of biometric data and other personal data involved in the use of AI systems for biometric identification, other than in connection to the use of ‘real-time’ remote biometric identification systems in publicly accessible spaces as regulated by this Regulation should continue to comply with all requirements resulting from Article 9
