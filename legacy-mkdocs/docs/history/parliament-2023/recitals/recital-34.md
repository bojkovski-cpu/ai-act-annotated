# Recital 34

‘emotion recognition system’ means an AI system for the purpose of identifying or inferring emotions , thoughts, states of mind or intentions of individuals or groups on the basis of their biometric and biometric-based data; Amendment 192 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 35
