# Recital 35

‘biometric categorisation means assigning natural persons to specific categories, or inferring their characteristics and attributes on the basis of their biometric or biometric-based data, or which can be inferred from such data ; Amendment 193 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 36
