# Recital 41

‘law enforcement’ means activities carried out by law enforcement authorities or on their behalf for the prevention, investigation, detection or prosecution of criminal offences or the execution of criminal penalties, including the safeguarding against and the prevention of threats to public security; Amendment 197 Proposal for a regulation Article 3 – paragraph 1 – point 42
