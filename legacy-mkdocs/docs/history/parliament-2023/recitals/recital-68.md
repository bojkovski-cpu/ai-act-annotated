# Recital 68

Under certain conditions, rapid availability of innovative technologies may be crucial for health and safety of persons , the environment and climate change and for society as a whole. It is thus appropriate that under exceptional reasons of protection of life and health of natural persons , environmental protection and the protection of critical infrastructure , Member States could authorise the placing on the market or putting into service of AI systems which have not undergone a conformity assessment.
