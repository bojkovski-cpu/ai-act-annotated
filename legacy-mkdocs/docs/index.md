## Welcome

Welcome to the **Annotated EU AI Act**, a comprehensive reference for understanding and implementing the European Union's groundbreaking artificial intelligence regulation. This site provides the complete official text along with cross-references, commentary, and practical guidance.

## What is the AI Act?

The **EU Artificial Intelligence Act** is the world's first comprehensive legal framework for artificial intelligence. It:

- 🎯 Establishes harmonized rules for AI systems across the EU
- ⚖️ Balances innovation with protection of fundamental rights
- 🚫 Prohibits certain high-risk AI practices
- 📋 Sets requirements for high-risk AI systems
- 🌐 Promotes trustworthy and human-centric AI

## Important Dates

The AI Act has a phased implementation timeline:

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| **1 August 2024** | AI Act enters into force |
| **2 February 2025** | Prohibited practices (Article 5) apply |
| **2 August 2025** | General-purpose AI rules (Chapter V) apply |
| **2 August 2026** | High-risk AI obligations (Chapter III) apply |
| **2 August 2027** | Full implementation for all provisions |

    Organizations should begin preparing now for the upcoming compliance obligations. See our [Implementation Timeline](guidance/implementation-timeline.md) for details.
